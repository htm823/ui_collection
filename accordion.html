Q: Who is Sherlock Holmes, and why is he so closely associated with Britain?
A: Sherlock Holmes is a fictional consulting detective created by British author Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. He is closely associated with Britain because most of his stories are set in London, particularly at 221B Baker Street, and they reflect Victorian-era British society, culture, and values such as logic, observation, and rational thinking.
